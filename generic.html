Hi, I'm Babafunso — I do not just uncover insights, I turn them into decisions that drive impact.
Because in a world overflowing with data, clarity is power — luckily, I bring both.

I’m a data-driven storyteller with a focus on Business Intelligence, turning complex datasets into insights that drive clarity, speed, and smarter decisions. Backed by nearly two years of experience in the telecommunications and banking sectors, I specialize in transforming data into strategic advantage—crafting high-impact dashboards, uncovering deep insights through indepth analysis, and converting trends into actionable business outcomes that drive measurable results.

I work with SQL, Python, Power BI, and Excel to analyze, visualize, and present data in ways that matter. My strength lies in bridging the gap between data and decision-making — aligning technical analysis with business context to deliver insights that lead to action.

From crafting executive dashboards to building predictive models or surfacing trends buried deep in the data, I tackle each project with a problem-solving mindset and a drive to turn complexity into insight.
